`fossable.org` is home to a group of software tools that generally help users
maintain self-control of their computing experiences in various ways. To
highlight the main ones:

- [sandpolis](https://www.fossable.org/projects/sandpolis/) manages your virtual
  estate.

- [goldboot](https://www.fossable.org/projects/goldboot/) builds and installs
  customized golden images of your OS.

- [outpost](https://www.fossable.org/projects/outpost/) allows you to self-host
  applications on the public Internet, while still having some conveniences of
  the cloud.

- [turbine](https://www.fossable.org/projects/turbine/) distributes
  cryptocurrency to contributors of git repos.

- [workset](https://www.fossable.org/projects/workset/) manages your git
  workspaces, making it easy to control where your repos are hosted.

Fossable is not a business or foundation of any kind. We don't have trademarks,
copyrights, patents, or licenses. The name doesn't mean anything special; it's
just a software-y word that sounds positive.

If any of that seems interesting, join one of the projects above! Contributions
are the best way to engage, but I guess you can also use
[Github Sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors/cilki) as well.
